What’s Available on the Gigachad Network?

All services listed below are self-hosted by the Gigachad Network—your data stays private and under your control. All services are Ad-free and payment free. More information about each service can be found in Articles and Tutorials.

  • Jellyfin: Media streaming server for movies and TV shows. Your own private Netflix alternative.
  • Vaultwarden (coming soon): Password manager. Securely store and manage your passwords.
  • VPN: Secure VPN access using WireGuard. Protect your privacy and access your home network from anywhere.
  • NextCloud: Personal cloud storage. Sync and share files, calendars, and contacts across your devices.
  • AudiobookShelf: Audiobook and podcast streaming. Listen to your audiobooks and podcasts anywhere.
  • Immich: Cloud photo management. Backup, organize, and share your photos and videos.
  • Navidrome: Music streaming server. Stream your music collection to any device, anywhere.
  • Gigachad AI (coming soon): AI chat and tools. Powerful AI features for productivity and fun.
  • Gigachad Email (coming soon): Private email service. Take control of your email privacy.
  • Our Own Game Servers: Play on some of our own private servers. Including Minecraft, Factorio, and Valheim
  • Server Hosting (Toast Host): Start your own game server with Toast Host. Easy, fast, and reliable game server hosting.
