Setting up AudioBookShelf

What is AudioBookShelf

AudioBookShelf is a self-hosted audiobook and podcast server. It lets you stream your audiobooks and podcasts to any device. This tutorial will guide you through setting up the AudioBookShelf app on your phone.

Step 1: Download the AudioBookShelf App

You can download the AudioBookShelf app or forks of it from:
• Shelf Player (App Store, $5) – a fork for iOS
• Google Play (official AudioBookShelf app)
• Lissen (F-Droid) – a lightweight, simple audiobook player

Step 2: App Login

Open the AudioBookShelf app and enter http://gigachad.website:30067 into the Server box. Enter the account details that were assigned to you.

If you don't have an account, please contact the server administrators to create one for you.

Step 3: App Setup

To browse and listen to your audiobooks and podcasts, use the navigation options in the app. You can also adjust your settings by clicking on the gear icon in the top right corner.

Step 4: Enjoy AudioBookShelf

You can now sign in on any device or web browser and access your audiobooks and podcasts. If you have any problems, questions, or audiobook requests, contact Ben_bone_ on Discord or through our support email v4bkmt0jv@mozmail.com.
