Setting up Navidrome

What is Navidrome?

Navidrome is a self-hosted music server that lets you stream your music collection to any device, anywhere. It supports multiple users, playlists, and works with a variety of Subsonic-compatible apps. This tutorial will guide you through connecting to Navidrome and enjoying your music library.

Step 1: Choose a Music App

Navidrome works with many Subsonic-compatible apps. Here are some popular options:
• SubStreamer (Android)
• Tempo (F-Droid)
• SubStreamer (iOS)
• Amperfy Music (iOS)
• Or use the Navidrome Web Player directly in your browser.

Step 2: App Login

Open your chosen app and enter http://gigachad.website:30043 as the server address. For iOS you may need to pick your server type, choose the Subsonic option. Use the account details provided to you.

If you don't have an account, please contact the server administrators to create one for you.

Step 3: Enjoy Your Music

You can now stream your music collection from any device or web browser. If you have any problems, questions, or music requests, contact Ben_bone_ on Discord or through our support email v4bkmt0jv@mozmail.com.
