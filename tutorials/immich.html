Setting up Immich

What is Immich

Immich is a self-hosted photo and video backup solution for your mobile device. Put simply, it is a free version of Google Photos. This tutorial will guide you through setting up the Immich app on your phone.

Step 1: Download the Immich App

Download Immich from: App Store, F-Droid, or Google Play.

Step 2: App Login

Open the Immich app and enter http://gigachad.website:30041 into the Server endpoint URL. Enter the account details that were assigned to you.

If you don't have an account, please contact the server administrators to create one for you.

Step 3: App Setup

To back up photos and videos, go to the cloud button in the top right corner. Once there, you can click upload to start the process. To set up auto backup, click on the gear icon in the top right corner. Here you can adjust your settings to your desire.

Step 4: Enjoy Immich

You can now sign in on any device or web browser and access your photos. If you have any problems or questions, contact Ben_bone_ on Discord or through our support email v4bkmt0jv@mozmail.com.
