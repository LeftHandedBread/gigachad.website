Setting up Gigachad AI

What is Gigachad AI?

Gigachad AI is our self-hosted Open WebUI instance for AI chat and tools. This page covers quick access and basic setup.

Quick Setup Guide

Step 1: Choose Your App

Download a Gigachad AI-compatible app:

  • iOS: Conduit (App Store) - $4
  • Android: Conduit (Google Play)
  • Web Browser: Visit ai.gigachad.website directly (no app needed)

Step 2: Connect to the Server

  1. Open the app or web client
  2. Enter server URL: https://ai.gigachad.website (if prompted)
  3. Log in with your account credentials

Don't have an account? Contact the server administrators to create one for you.

Step 3: Explore the Interface

Once logged in, you'll see:

  • Chat: Ask questions and continue conversations
  • Model selector: Pick the model that fits your task
  • History: Reopen previous chats anytime
