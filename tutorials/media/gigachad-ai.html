Setting up Gigachad AI

What is Gigachad AI?

Gigachad AI is a self-hosted AI platform that provides chat capabilities and various AI tools. Powered by Open WebUI, it gives you access to powerful AI models for productivity, creativity, and entertainment. This tutorial will help you get started with using the AI features.

Quick Setup Guide

Step 1: Access the Interface

Visit ai.gigachad.website directly in your web browser—no app needed!

In a web browser:

  1. Visit ai.gigachad.website
  2. Log in with your account credentials

On Android (Conduit app):

Prefer a mobile app? You can use Conduit on Android to access your Gigachad AI account with a native chat experience. Add your server URL (https://ai.gigachad.website), sign in with the same credentials, and start chatting from your phone.

Download Conduit on Google Play

Don't have an account? Contact the server administrators to create one for you.

Step 2: Explore the Interface

Once logged in, you'll see:

  • Chat Interface: A conversation area where you can interact with AI models
  • Models Dropdown: Choose from different AI models based on your needs
  • Quick Actions: Buttons for common tasks like generating images, coding assistance, and more
  • Conversation History: Access previous conversations and continue where you left off

Tips for Better Results:

  • Be specific and detailed in your prompts
  • Provide context when asking questions
  • Try different models for different tasks (some excel at coding, others at creative writing)
  • Use conversation history to build on previous discussions

Step 4: Manage Your Account

Profile Settings:

  • Access your profile by clicking your username in the top right
  • Manage your API keys and preferences
  • View your usage statistics and history
