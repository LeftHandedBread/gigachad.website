Setting up AudioBookShelf

What is AudioBookShelf?

AudioBookShelf is a self-hosted audiobook and podcast server. Stream your audiobooks and podcasts to any deviceâ€”phone, tablet, or computer. This tutorial will walk you through getting started with AudioBookShelf.

Quick Setup Guide

Step 1: Choose Your App

Download an AudioBookShelf-compatible app:

  • iOS: Shelf Player ($5, one-time purchase)
  • iOS: Still for Audiobookshelf - Free with in-app purchases
  • Android: AudioBookShelf (Google Play) - Free
  • Android (f-droid): Lissen (F-Droid) - Lightweight, open-source
  • Web Browser: Visit listen.gigachad.website directly (no app needed)

Step 2: Connect to the Server

In the app:

  1. Open the AudioBookShelf app
  2. Enter the server URL: https://listen.gigachad.website
  3. Log in with your account credentials

In a web browser:

  1. Visit listen.gigachad.website
  2. Log in with your account credentials

Don't have an account? Contact the server administrators to create one for you.

Step 3: Start Listening

Once logged in, you can:

  • Browse the audiobook and podcast library
  • Download content for offline listening
  • Sync your progress across all devices
  • Adjust playback speed and skip silences
