Setting up Navidrome

What is Navidrome?

Navidrome is a self-hosted music server that lets you stream your personal music library to your phone, tablet, and computer. It supports multiple users, playlists, and Subsonic-compatible apps. This tutorial will help you connect and start listening.

Quick Setup Guide

Step 1: Choose Your Access Method

Option A: Mobile/Desktop Apps (Subsonic-compatible)

Popular apps that work with Navidrome:

  • iOS: Amperfy
  • iOS: SubStreamer
  • Android: SubStreamer
  • Android: Symfonium (free trial, then a one-time payment of $7)
  • Android (F-Droid): Tempo

Option B: Web Browser

Step 2: Connect to the Server

In the app:

  1. Open your Subsonic-compatible app
  2. Add a new server/account
  3. Enter the server URL: http://music.gigachad.website
  4. If asked for server type, choose Subsonic
  5. Log in with your account credentials

In a web browser:

  1. Visit music.gigachad.website
  2. Log in with your account credentials

Don't have an account? Contact the server administrators to create one for you.

Step 3: Browse & Listen

Once logged in, you can:

  • Browse your library by artist, album, genre, and songs
  • Create and manage playlists
  • Search your library quickly from any device
  • Stream in multiple formats (MP3/FLAC and more)
