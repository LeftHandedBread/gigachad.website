Setting up Jellyfin

What is Jellyfin

Jellyfin is a free, open-source media server that lets you organize, manage, and stream your movies, TV shows, music, and more to any device. With Jellyfin, you can create your own personal Netflix at home, accessible from your phone, tablet, or computer. This tutorial will guide you through setting up the Jellyfin app on your phone.

Step 1: Download the Jellyfin App

Download Jellyfin from: App Store, F-Droid, or Google Play.

Step 2: App Login

Open the Jellyfin app and enter http://gigachad.website:30013 into the Server endpoint URL. Enter the account details that were assigned to you.

If you don't have an account, please contact the server administrators to create one for you.

Step 3: App Setup

To browse and stream your media, use the navigation options in the app. You can also adjust your settings by clicking on the gear icon in the top right corner.

Step 4: Enjoy Jellyfin

You can now sign in on any device or web browser and access the available movies and shows. If you have any problems, questions, or movie requests, contact Ben_bone_ on Discord or through our support email v4bkmt0jv@mozmail.com.
