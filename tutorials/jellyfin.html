Setting up Jellyfin

What is Jellyfin?

Jellyfin is a free, open-source media server for streaming movies, TV shows, music, and more to any device. Think of it as your own personal Netflix—accessible from your phone, tablet, computer, or smart TV. This tutorial will get you started with Jellyfin.

Quick Setup Guide

Step 1: Choose Your Access Method

Option A: Mobile App (Recommended)

Download the official Jellyfin app:

  • iOS: App Store
  • Android: Google Play
  • Android (Alternative): F-Droid

Option B: Web Browser

Visit watch.gigachad.website directly—no app needed!

Step 2: Connect to the Server

In the app:

  1. Open the Jellyfin app
  2. Tap "Add Server"
  3. Enter the server URL: http://watch.gigachad.website
  4. Log in with your account credentials

In a web browser:

  1. Visit watch.gigachad.website
  2. Log in with your account credentials

Don't have an account? Contact the server administrators to create one for you.

Step 3: Browse & Stream

Once logged in, you can:

  • Browse movies, TV shows, and music organized by library
  • Use filters and search to find content quickly
  • Resume watching from where you left off on any device
  • Create watchlists and mark favorites
  • Adjust quality settings based on your connection

Request Content

Want to request a movie or show? Use Jellyseer to submit content requests. You can log in with the same Jellyfin credentials!
